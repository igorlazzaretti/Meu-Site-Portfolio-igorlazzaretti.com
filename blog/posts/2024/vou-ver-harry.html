I'm happy to anounce that I'm going to watch Harry Potter and the Prisioner of Azkaban in theaters!

Stop, stop, stop! (Read in João Kleber's voice) No, you are not in June 04, 2004. Twenty years after its debut, the movie will return to the cinema and I'll be there. Of course, I've watched the movie over a million (seriously, so many) times already. However, seeing it on a giant screen will once again be a unique experience. Alongside fellow fans in their thirties, I'll pay attention to details created by Eduardo Lima, the Brazilian designer whose work has been immortalized.

Harry Potter is an entertainment masterpiece that still inspires me today. It even inspired this blog. This blog will serve to hone my technology and English writing skills. Now, eagerly, I'll await the movie.

And now all my devices are in the same mood.
