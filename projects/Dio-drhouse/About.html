About


"House, M.D." is a popular American medical drama television series that aired from 2004 to 2012. The show revolves around the character Dr. Gregory House, played by Hugh Laurie, who is a brilliant but unconventional and cynical medical genius. House leads a team of diagnosticians at the fictional Princeton–Plainsboro Teaching Hospital in New Jersey.

The hospital itself is a teaching hospital associated with a university, and it serves as the primary setting for the series. Throughout the show, viewers get a glimpse into various departments and areas within the hospital, including the emergency room, patient rooms, diagnostic imaging, operating rooms, and administrative offices.
